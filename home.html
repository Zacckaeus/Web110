The MCU

The Marvel Cinaemtic Universe (MCU)

The MCU first started in 2008 with the release of Iron Man (2008) and consists of:

  • 35 Feature Length Films
  • 11 Television Shows
  • 17 Direct to Streaming series
  • 8 'One Shot' short films

The MCU as it has been released consists of 5 differnet 'Phases', and 2 'Sagas'; however, the current roadmap from marvel studios is a total of 6 Phases.
